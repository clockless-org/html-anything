What this proves

Each of the 5 pages was generated from a single one-line prompt (e.g., "Cupertino neighborhood deep dive with schools matrix and lead capture"). In the real product, this becomes a new "Pages" section inside the existing Assets tab:

  • Sharon picks a template type (lead magnet, neighborhood guide, drip chapter, client portal, …).
  • She types or speaks one prompt.
  • AI generates the full HTML using her brand voice and her real data (CRM stage, current listings, school zones).
  • One click publishes to sharonwangrealtor.com/p/<slug> (public) or her private client URL (drip + portal).
